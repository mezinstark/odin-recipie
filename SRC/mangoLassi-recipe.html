Mango Lassi

Learn how to make mango lassi, the perfect summer drink any mango lover will love. Mango lassi is a sweet and refreshing treat only needs 6 ingredients! â€”Namrata Telugu, Terre Haute, Indiana

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup fat-free plain yogurt
  • 1 medium mango, peeled and cubed
  • 2 cups ice cubes
  • 3 tablespoon sugar
  • 5 fresh mint leaves
  • 2 cardamom pods, crushed, optional

  1. In a blender, combine the yogurt, mango, ice, sugar, mint leaves and cardamom pods if desired. Cover and process for 30-60 seconds or until blended. Pour into two chilled glasses; serve immediately.
